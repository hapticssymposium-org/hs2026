Work-in-Progress Poster Session

Last updated: April 5, 2024.

Monday, April 8, 5:00 p.m. - 6:30 p.m.
- Vista del Mar

The Work-in-Progress track invites authors to share late-breaking results with the research community in a non-archival format. WIP posters will be presented during a WIP poster session 5:00 to 6:30pm on Monday, April 8 in a joint session alongside IEEE Transactions on Haptics Poster session.
